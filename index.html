About Us

Neonox gas tech Projects Division specializes in high-precision pipeline fabrication, orbital welding, TIG welding projects, and industrial manifold installation for gas distribution systems. We provide turnkey project solutions including design, fabrication, installation, testing, and commissioning for process industries dealing with high-purity and hazardous gases. We support industries requiring clean, accurate, and leak-proof welding for small-diameter tubes from 1/8” to 1/2”, stainless steel and special alloy pipelines.
